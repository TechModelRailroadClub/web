Building 20 Live Presentation Information

The presentation "TMRC - the future" was held Friday March 27th, 1998 at 4:05pm in N52-118, TMRC's new clubroom. The presentation was broadcast live over the internet, a first among any railroad club. This presentation was the first of many steps the club wants to take in order to combine model-railroading and the internet.

System Requirements

The presentation was recorded using RealNetworks video streaming. In order to view the saved presentation you need the following:
  • Real Player 5.0
  • Network Connection at 56.6kbps minimum
  • Pentium Computer (or equivalent)

You can get Real Player for free
