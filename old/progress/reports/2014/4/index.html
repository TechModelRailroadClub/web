This page describes work accomplished on the new TMRC layout during the month of April 2014, as taken from the descriptions posted to the club mailing list.
April 16

Dillon thought that the workbench needed a new coat of paint, and he enlisted new member Natasha to help apply it.
John M came to supervise but ended up wielding a brush.
Meanwhile John P was cutting plywood for the new display setup, out in the hallway to make noise and dust less of a problem.
The dust did pile up.
Alex is very pleased with the new display.
Then (actually on a later evening) he "planted some people" at Killianport.
People like this, as delivered from the West Coast Shops (thanks David!)
They end up like this.
Here's the new display system, as programmed by Prajwal.


April 26

Spring 2014 open house was today! Turnout was good with a lot of people swinging by thanks to the Cambridge Science Festival. Here are some the picture for those of you who could not make it.

The new display system was put through its paces at the open house today!
The streetcar made another apparence and ran smoothly all open house long
Alex L. was the dispatcher for the first few hours of the open house
New member Alex P. also ended up dispatching trains for the open house
Problems with trains were dealt with swiftly
As always, old members made an apparence
But of course the most important part of the open house were all the kids and their families
Models were admired by all
