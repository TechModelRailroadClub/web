This page describes work accomplished on the new TMRC layout during the month of December 2013, as taken from the descriptions posted to the club mailing list.
December 14

New ties for new track! A review of the likely operation of the paper mill showed how much we need a lead/engine escape track. That's what's going in here.


December 21

Alex has been building trees.
And working with greenery--it is a messy job.
John P can't stop laying out complicated track in the paper mill area. Unfortunately, it's not the 40" template that's getting most use.


December 28

Bizarre tracklaying continues at the paper mill.
