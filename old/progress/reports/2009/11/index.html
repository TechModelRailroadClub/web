This page describes work accomplished on the new TMRC layout during the month of November 2009, as taken from the descriptions posted to the club mailing list.
November 25

We had an excellent Open House on the 21st, and maybe someone will post pictures from it.

And there's Progress!


New member Alex Keck worked on assembling a brass switch stand kit. With tonight's effort, he has reached his required 10 hours of work for the club, and we gave him the secret combination to the clubroom door.
Veteran member John McNamara continued construction of the steel warehouse in East Bermannville.
Michael Stunes has more freight cars to put together--tonight he was building a vintage reefer.
John Purbrick built some more of the wall below F-Yard, ready for planting weeds at ground level.
