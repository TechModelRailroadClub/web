This page describes work accomplished on the new TMRC layout during the month of June 2012, as taken from the descriptions posted to the club mailing list.
June 13

Trimming plywood for the next section of Level 2.
Plywood in place.
The previous section caught a lot of the construction clutter.
Berkmannville has become very dark, but we have a plan to deal with that.


June 20

The new plywood section is now bolted down, via T-nuts.
Progress on Andy's bridge across F-Yard.
The future Central Vermont tracks.
A view from the back of N52-118.


June 27

Tracks drawn onto the new plywood section.
Next door at MITERS, John M supervised Quentin's work on the new grade crossing signal (see May 23).
Quentin finished with a remarkable number of fingers left intact.
The CV track has been extended, and has a turnout for a layover siding.
Andy did more work on his bridge.
There is a support structure underneath, as well as a weathered handrail above.
