This page describes work accomplished on the new TMRC layout during the month of July 2010, as taken from the descriptions posted to the club mailing list.
July 7

Alex has decided that maybe a hobby servo would be better for his project than a stepper.
Ballasting is complete at the chemical plant, and we'll be spiking rails this coming weekend. This is Code 55 rail, painted with gray and brown because it didn't come pre-weathered.
John P wrote some software to read out the location and actions of all the cabs.
John M added gravel around the picnic tables on his industrial lawn.
We've never shown a picture of the bridge over the stream in Berkmannville after the area was finished and planted with greenery. It looks like this.
