Everyone is welcome at our open houses. Come see trains running, signals working, and more. The layout and control system continue to grow; you'll see something new every Open House.

We typically hold an open house twice a year, once in late April and again in November. Our open houses are held Saturdays, and are split into afternoon and evening sessions. The two sessions are basically the same, so there is no advantage to attending one rather than the other. However, the afternoon session tends to be more crowded.

Open house information is posted here as soon as schedules are set. Be sure to check this page for changes to the scheduled date and time as the information is subject to change. If you would like an email notice a week or two before each open house, you can sign up for our announcement list by going here. This list will only be used by the club to announce open houses and major club events (you can expect to get 2-6 messages a year from us).
