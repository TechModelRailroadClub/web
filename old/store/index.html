Welcome to TMRC's Online Store

TMRC is a student group at MIT, and the TMRC Store is one of our few fundraising activities. Building a model railroad is very challenging. Every time you buy something from our store, you help us in building the layout.

This page shows all of our current products. Our current listings include:

  • TMRC T-Shirts: Men et tracus, available in S, M, L, XL, and XXL.
  • Book: The Tech Model Railroad of MIT: The First Fifty Years by Joseph Onorato ('66) & Mark Schupack ('53)
All of the products you see here were designed by TMRC members.

We are a non-profit organization, and any additional donation is appreciated.

We accept payment by check or money order in US funds only. Sorry, no credit card orders at this time.

If you are an out-of-town fan of TMRC, we do ship to your home address. Please include 10.00 USD shipping and handling per address to your order and send your request to tmrc-web@mit.edu and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
